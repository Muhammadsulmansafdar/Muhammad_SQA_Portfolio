The Mission

I was tasked with breaking the synchronization logic of Canva’s most ambitious architectural shift: Total Offline Freedom.

Testing an "Offline-First" experience isn't about checking buttons—it’s about simulating the chaotic reality of the modern user. My work focused on the high-friction moments when a design moves from a persistent server state to a volatile local state. I took full ownership of the end-to-end verification across the Windows/macOS Desktop Apps and Mobile ecosystems to ensure that "Airplane Mode" never meant "Data Loss."

Chaos Engineering

Simulated flaky, throttled, and intermittent internet transitions. I pushed the sync engine to its limits to see if it could survive a hard-close during a background upload or a session hand-off between devices.

State Persistence Logic

Verified the Mandatory Reload protocols and local cache integrity. If a user added a high-resolution video or an audio track while offline, I ensured the "Conflict Resolution" logic favored the user's hard work over server errors.
